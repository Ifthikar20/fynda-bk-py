Cookie Policy

Last updated: February 2, 2026

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit websites. They help us remember your preferences, understand how you use our platform, and improve your experience.

Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Essential Required for basic site functionality, authentication, and security Session / 1 year
Analytics Help us understand how visitors interact with our site (Mixpanel) 1 year
Affiliate Track referrals to partner retailers for commission attribution 30 days

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly and cannot be disabled:

  • Authentication: Keep you logged in
  • Security: Protect against fraud and unauthorized access
  • Preferences: Remember your cookie consent choice

Analytics Cookies

We use Mixpanel to understand how users interact with Fynda. This helps us:

  • Identify popular features and content
  • Improve user experience
  • Fix issues and bugs

You can opt out of analytics cookies through our cookie banner.

Affiliate & Third-Party Cookies

When you click on product links, retailers may set cookies to:

  • Track your visit from Fynda
  • Attribute purchases for affiliate commissions
  • Personalize their shopping experience

These cookies are set by third parties (e.g., Awin, ShareASale) and are subject to their privacy policies.

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can manage your cookie preferences in several ways:

  • Cookie Banner: Use our consent banner when you first visit
  • Browser Settings: Block or delete cookies in your browser
  • Opt-Out Links: Use third-party opt-out tools

Note: Disabling certain cookies may affect site functionality.
